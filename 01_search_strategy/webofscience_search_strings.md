## Notes on year filter

Only year-level box-check filters were available via the Web of Science interface; no month- or day-level filters were applied.

- No records were retrieved prior to 2018 in any iteration; therefore the box-check range 2018–2025 captures all records that would have been eligible under the prespecified 2015-01-01 to 2025-06-30 window.
- The search yielded no records published after June 30, 2025 in the retrieved set, so no post-2025-06-30 records required manual exclusion.
