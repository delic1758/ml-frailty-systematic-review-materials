# PubMed — Search Strategy

**Database:** PubMed (NCBI / U.S. National Library of Medicine)
**Search execution date:** July 4, 2025
**Records retrieved:** 65

---

## Boolean search string

```
("Frailty" OR "Pre-frailty" OR "Cognitive Frailty" OR "Physical Frailty")
AND
("Machine learning" OR "Artificial Intelligence" OR "Deep learning"
 OR "Predictive Model" OR "Prediction Model" OR "Classification Model")
AND
("Community-dwelling older adults" OR "Community-living older adults"
 OR "Community-dwelling elderly" OR "Older adults living in community")
```

## Filters applied

| Filter | Value |
|---|---|
| Publication date | 2015-01-01 to 2025-06-30 |
| Language | English |
| Species | Humans |

## Conceptual domain structure

Search built around three conceptual domains combined with AND; terms within each domain combined with OR.

- **Domain 1 — Frailty constructs:** Frailty, Pre-frailty, Cognitive Frailty, Physical Frailty
- **Domain 2 — Machine learning:** Machine learning, Artificial Intelligence, Deep learning, Predictive Model, Prediction Model, Classification Model
- **Domain 3 — Population:** Community-dwelling older adults, Community-living older adults, Community-dwelling elderly, Older adults living in community

## Notes

- Field tags were not added beyond the default "All Fields" search to maximize sensitivity at this stage; specificity was achieved through downstream eligibility screening.
- The same conceptual three-domain structure was applied to all primary databases (Embase, Web of Science, Scopus) and to the supplementary IEEE Xplore / ACM Digital Library search.
